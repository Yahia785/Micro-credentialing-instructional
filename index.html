Blockchain-Based Micro-Credentialing for Engineering Education

Building a portfolio of verified programming competencies beyond traditional grades

Welcome to the Pilot Program

We are piloting a new approach to programming assessment that documents verified programming skills in a way that extends beyond traditional grades. This initiative is part of a larger long-term vision for engineering education.

Traditional grading systems reduce your work to a number — a GPA, a percentage, a letter. While grades matter, they do not fully capture what you are capable of building or solving. In industry, what ultimately matters is demonstrable skill.

Why Participate?

  • Earn Bonus Points: Successful completion of the onboarding round earns bonus points on your midterm exam
  • Build Verified Credentials: Demonstrate specific technical skills with blockchain-verified credentials
  • Industry-Relevant Portfolio: Create a permanent, independently verifiable record of demonstrated competencies
  • Beyond Course Completion: Show what you can actually build and solve, not just that you took a course

How It Works

This platform uses blockchain technology as the infrastructure for credential issuance. Blockchain provides an immutable, tamper-resistant ledger — meaning once a credential is issued, it cannot be altered or fabricated. It becomes a permanent, independently verifiable record of demonstrated skill.

Your credentials represent verified academic achievement and technical proficiency. Their value is skill-driven, not market-driven.
