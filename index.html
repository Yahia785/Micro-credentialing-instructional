Blockchain-Based Micro-Credentialing for Engineering Education

Building a portfolio of verified programming competencies beyond traditional grades

Welcome to the Pilot Program

We are piloting a new approach to programming assessment that documents verified programming skills in a way that extends beyond traditional grades. This initiative is part of a larger long-term vision for engineering education.

Traditional grading systems reduce your work to a number — a GPA, a percentage, a letter. While grades matter, they do not fully capture what you are capable of building or solving. In industry, what ultimately matters is demonstrable skill.

Why Participate?

Earn Bonus Points

Successful completion of the onboarding round and bonus assessments earn bonus points on your midterm exams.

Shape the Future

Your participation and feedback directly influence the development of this system and may impact how technical skills are credentialed in engineering education.
