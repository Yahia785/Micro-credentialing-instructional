❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions. A reset link will be sent to your @vt.edu email address.

What if I miss the onboarding deadline?

The onboarding round is specifically timed to earn bonus points on your midterm. Missing the deadline means you won't receive the bonus points, but you can still participate in future assessments.

Can I retake an assessment if I fail?

Most assessments allow only one submission. However, future rounds of similar assessments may be offered. Contact course staff for specific policies.

My webcam isn't working. What should I do?

First, check the troubleshooting guide for camera permission issues. If the problem persists, submit a support ticket with details about your browser, operating system, and the exact error message.

How long does credential issuance take?

After you successfully pass an assessment, course staff will review your proctoring recordings. Once approved, credentials are typically issued within 1-2 weeks and will appear in your Credentials tab on the platform.

Who can view my proctoring recordings?

Only authorized course staff (Dr. Nazhand-Ali and Yahia Tawfik) can access your recordings. They are used solely for academic integrity verification and are not shared with anyone else.

🚨 Urgent Issues During Assessment

If you experience a critical technical failure during an active proctored assessment:

  1. Do not close the browser or refresh the page
  2. Take a screenshot of the error message
  3. Note the exact time the issue occurred
  4. Submit an emergency support ticket immediately
  5. Email course staff directly with "URGENT - Assessment Issue" in the subject line

Emergency Contact: yahiatawfik@vt.edu

Include your full name, the assessment name, and a description of the problem.
